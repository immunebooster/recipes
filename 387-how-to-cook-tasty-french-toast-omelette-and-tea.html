French toast, omelette and tea step by step

  1. These are the ingredients used. Chop onion,red pepper and blend scotch bonnet..
  2. Break egg in a bowl whisk and pour milk,mix well and then add sugar and salt whisk again and keep aside..
  3. Heat a pan,add butter. When it melts,take the bread slice and dip in the egg mixture,coat both sides evenly with the mixture then place in the hot pan..
  4. Fry and flip both sides when it turn to golden..Remove when both sides are golden..
  5. Omelette.... Break eggs in a bowl,add chopped onion,red pepper,Maggi and blended scotch bonnet and give it a good whisk..
  6. Add oil in a pan and pour egg mixture. Flip when brown/golden, then remove when both sides are golden.
  7. Finally,ur breakfast is readyyyyy.
  8. You can serve it with a hot tea. Enyoyyy.
