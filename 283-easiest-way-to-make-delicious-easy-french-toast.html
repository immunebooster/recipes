Ingredients of Easy french toast

  1. Prepare 4 slices of sandwich bread (thicker).
  2. You need 2 of whole eggs.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of milk.
  4. You need 1 tbsp of Honey / maple syrup / nutella.
  5. You need 1 tsp of sugar powder.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of oil/butter.

Easy french toast step by step

  1. Beat eggs in a wide plate or beat eggs in a bowl then transfer it to a plate. It is easier to dip the sandwich bread in the beaten egg. Add 2 tbsp milk to make the eggs thinner..
  2. Heat a non-stick pan. add a dash of oil / butter into the pan..
  3. Dip a slice of bread into the beaten egg both side. You may dip it longer or just to cover the sandwich bread..
  4. Transfer the sandwich bread into heated pan, bake it for 1 minute each side until golden brown..
  5. Continue to bake it with the rest of sandwich..
  6. Serve it warm with any condiments that you love such as nutella, peanut butter, maple syrup, honey, powder sugar, sliced bananas, berries..
