Ingredients of Oatmeal and peppermint soap

  1. Prepare 420 g of olive oil.
  2. Prepare 150 g of coconut oil.
  3. You need 30 g of cocoa butter.
  4. Prepare 81 g of LYE (caustic soda).
  5. It's 228 g of water.
  6. You need 1/2 cup of oatmeal.
  7. Prepare 25 ml of peppermint essential oil.

Oatmeal and peppermint soap step by step

  1. Use a big metallic or glass bowl and place it in the sink. For this step wear glasses and gloves. Pour water in the bowl then slowly the LYE. Stir. The liquid will fume and the bowl became hot. Leave it..
  2. Lipoprotein a pot place the oils and fats, heat until everything will dissolve.
  3. Pour the LYE solution in the pot with the oils. Use gloves and glasses. Blend with a stick blender for 5-10 minutes until it becames like a custard.
  4. Add the oatmeal and essential oil. Blend. Pour into a mold. It will harden in 24h..
