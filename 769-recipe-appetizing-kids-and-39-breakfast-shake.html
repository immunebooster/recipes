Ingredients of Kids ' Breakfast shake

  1. You need 1/2 cup of cooked oatmeal (old-fashioned or quick-cooking).
  2. You need 1 cup of milk, divided.
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp of Chocolate - flavored syrup.
  4. It's 1 tbsp of granulated sugar.
  5. Prepare 1/2 of banana, peeled.
  6. It's 1 tbsp of chunky or smooth peanut butter.

Kids ' Breakfast shake step by step

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine cooked oatmeal, 3/4 cup milk, chocolate syrup and sugar; mix well..
  2. Pour into an ice cube tray; freeze. Remove frozen cubes from ice cube try and store in plastic bag in freezer until needed..
  3. When ready to make the shake, combine banana and remaining 1/4 cup milk in container of electric blender. Blend until smooth..
  4. Remove frozen oatmeal cubes from freezer. Turn blender on medium-high speed; add frozen cubes to mixture in blender, one at a time, until all cubes are used and mixture is well blended..
  5. (Add a little more milk, if necessary for easier blending.) Add peanut butter; blend until well mixed..
