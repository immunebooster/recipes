Ingredients of Overnight porridge with coconut, lime and pineapple

  1. Prepare 40 of porridge oats.
  2. It's 200 g of greek yoghurt.
  3. It's 250 ml of light coconut milk.
  4. It's 2 tbsp of honey.
  5. You need 4 tbsp of desiccated coconut.
  6. It's 1 of tinned pineapple in juice (130g) drained weight.
  7. It's 1 of lime juice plus zest for decoration.
  8. Prepare 30 g of shelled pistachio nuts (could do with different nuts).

Overnight porridge with coconut, lime and pineapple instructions

  1. Mix greek yoghurt, coconut milk, lime juice and honey. Then add oats and stir. Add 2/3 chopped pineapple.
  2. Pour mixture into 2 bowls.
  3. Top it up with desiccated coconut, rest of pineapple chunks and crushed pistachio nuts.
