Ingredients of French toast Besan cheela sandwich

  1. Prepare 1 packet of Whole wheat bread.
  2. It's 1 up of Maida flour / All Purpose Flour.
  3. You need 1/2 cup of Semolina.
  4. It's 1 cup of Besan flour / Gram flour.
  5. Prepare as needed of Grated paneer.
  6. It's as needed of Butter.
  7. It's 1 each of Finely chopped onions,tomato.
  8. It's 1 of Shredded carrot.
  9. It's 1 teaspoon of pepper powder.
  10. It's 1 teaspoon of jeera powder / cumin powder.
  11. You need as needed of Lilltle coriander leaves.
  12. It's to taste of Salt.

French toast Besan cheela sandwich instructions

  1. Take a bowl,add maida flour,semolina and chopped tomatoes and onions.Then add shredded carrot, and lil coriander leaf,jeera powder and add salt,water and make a batter..
  2. Grate paneer and add besan flour,pepper powder,salt,water to make a batter of dosa consistency..
  3. Take bread slice and dip it in maida batter as shown below in the picture and toast it on tawa by adding butter.Now French toast is ready..
  4. On tawa make besan cheela just like how you make dosas but it should be small in size as we need to keep it in between bread slices.Now besan cheela is ready..
  5. Now take one slice of French toast and on top of it place besan cheela then add sliced onions,carrots, tomatoes and place one more French toast on top of it.Garnish it with coriander leaves and shredded carrots.serve it with sauce or mint chutney..
