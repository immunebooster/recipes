African breakfast sausages, pancakes, salad with kachumbari instructions

  1. Put 2 glasses of Wheat flour in a bowl.
  2. Add 3 glasses of water and stir.
  3. Add salt and sugar and Stir.
  4. Add eggs and mix throughly.
  5. Heat cooking oil in a pan and add small amount of mixture.
  6. Add small amount of oil on top of the pancake and turn let it cook all sides.
  7. When done repeat the process for all the pancake.
  8. Serve while hot with tea.
