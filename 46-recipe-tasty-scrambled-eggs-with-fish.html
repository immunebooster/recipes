Ingredients of Scrambled Eggs with Fish

  1. Prepare 1 of green chili, sliced.
  2. You need 2 of salted Fish fillets.
  3. Prepare 4 of eggs, beaten.
  4. You need to taste of all-purpose seasoning.
  5. You need 3 of garlic cloves, chopped.
  6. Prepare 1 of large onion, chopped.
  7. You need 1 bunch of parsley, chopped.
  8. It's to taste of freshly ground black pepper.
  9. You need 3 tablespoons of olive oil.

Scrambled Eggs with Fish instructions

  1. Add the fish to cold water overnight. Change the water 1-2 times..
  2. Shred the fillets into small pieces using your fingers..
  3. In a pan or wok, heat the olive oil..
  4. Add the onion, chilli and garlic and fry for about 3-5 minutes over a low-medium heat or until the onion has softened..
  5. Add the fish and fry for a further 3-5 minutes, stirring regularly..
  6. Add the parsley and the eggs..
  7. Add in the seasoning and black pepper. Stir regularly. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the eggs are cooked through..
