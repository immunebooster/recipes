Chilli and ginger immune boosting chicken instructions

  1. Marinate all the ingredients of the marinade and leave minimum 2 hours. Best overnight.
  2. On the day of cooking....first heat the pan. Spray 5 sprays of 1 calorie spray oil.
  3. Add chilli, ginger, garlic, coriander and stir for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the sliced onion and add the cumin seeds. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the tomatoes. Cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Add 200ml water and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
  7. Add the chicken and then add another 200ml water ensuring the chicken is fully covered.
  8. Cook on medium heat for 10 minutes with a lid on.
  9. Add garam masala by sprinkling on top and leave to cook for 5 minutes with lid on.
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves. And ready to serve!.
