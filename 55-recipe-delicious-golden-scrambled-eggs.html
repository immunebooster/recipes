Ingredients of Golden scrambled eggs

Golden scrambled eggs step by step

  1. Get your eggs and ingredients out and ready.
  2. Break the eggs into a small mixing bowl. Whisk well, then add salt, pepper, and the turmeric, (and milk if using for a cream creamier finish), and whisk again..
  3. Heat two tablespoons of butter in a pan. Keep it a light yellow, if it starts to go dark brown it may be burning and the heat too high..
  4. Put your eggs into the pan. Keep stirring to stop it sticking to the pan. After 2 minutes take it off the heat and grate your cheese. Put your pan back on the heat to finish cooking..
  5. When the egg is cooked, serve up and enjoy!.
