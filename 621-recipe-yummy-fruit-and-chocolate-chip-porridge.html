Fruit and Chocolate chip Porridge.

You can cook Fruit and Chocolate chip Porridge using 5 ingredients and 4 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Fruit and Chocolate chip Porridge

  1. Prepare 40 g of Porridge Oats.
  2. You need 280 ml of water/milk.
  3. It's Handful of Fresh Strawberries.
  4. Prepare Handful of Fresh Blueberries.
  5. It's Handful of Chocolate Chips.

Fruit and Chocolate chip Porridge step by step

  1. Add the oats and water/milk to a saucepan, bring to the boil and then reduce heat for 7-8 minutes.
  2. Take off heat.
  3. Sprinkle with fruit and chocolate chips.
  4. Serve.
