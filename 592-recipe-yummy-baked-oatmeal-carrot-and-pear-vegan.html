Ingredients of Baked oatmeal: carrot and pear - vegan

  7. It's 1-2 tsp of vanilla essence.
  8. It's 1 cup of grated carrots.
  9. You need 1 of pear, peeled and chopped.
  10. You need of Some dried cranberries and walnuts and/ or pumpkin seeds to sprinkle on top.
