Simple American pancakes.

You can cook Simple American pancakes using 9 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Simple American pancakes

  1. Prepare 120 g of Plain flour.
  2. You need 2 teaspoons of Baking powder.
  3. It's of Salt a small pinch.
  4. Prepare 2 Tablespoons of Caster sugar.
  5. Prepare 240 ml of Milk.
  6. Prepare 1 of Egg.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of Vanilla paste.
  8. Prepare 2 Tablespoons of Vegetable oil.
  9. Prepare 10 g of Butter.

Simple American pancakes instructions

  1. Put flour, baking powder, sugar and salt into a bowel and mix well..
  2. Get a measuring cup and put milk, egg and vanilla paste in it and mix well. Then add in the oil and mix it well again..
  3. Pour the liquid ingredients into the flour bowel and mix them well..
  4. Put some butter in a hot frying pan and add in some batter. When there are so many bubbles coming out, it’s time to flip..
  5. Cook another side side for a few seconds..
  6. It’s great to add any toppings and sauces. Enjoy..
