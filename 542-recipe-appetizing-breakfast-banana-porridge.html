Ingredients of Breakfast banana porridge

  1. It's 70 g of oats (we use jumbo for extra texture).
  2. Prepare 400 ml of milk or dairy-free(we like coconut or cashew).
  3. Prepare 2 of bananas (slightly overripe makes it sweeter).
  4. Prepare of Large dash of ground cinnamon.
  5. Prepare Dash of ground nutmeg.
  6. It's Handful of blueberries.

Breakfast banana porridge step by step

  1. Measure out the milk and oats into a pan..
  2. Put pan onto a medium heat. Slice the banana into small chunks and add to pan.
  3. Add the spices and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Once simmering turn the heat down a little and stir until the mixture thickens and the banana has almost completely disappeared.
  5. Spoon into bowls and garnish with blueberries. Enjoy!.
