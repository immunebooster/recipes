French toast step by step

  1. In a large bowl crack open 3 eggs and beat them..
  2. Next pour in the milk,sugar,vanilla extract,cinnamon powder and salt,stir again..
  3. Dip bread slices into the egg mixture,dredging them well on both sides..
  4. Place on a hot greased pan,cook for a few minutes until bottom of the breads start to get golden brown,flip the other side..
  5. Remove to a plate,serve warm with sugar syrup and a sprinkle of powdered sugar,.
