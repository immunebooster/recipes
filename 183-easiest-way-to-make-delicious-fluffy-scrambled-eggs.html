Fluffy scrambled eggs step by step

  1. Crack your eggs in a bowl and whisk. Add milk into your eggs and continue whisking..
  2. Put your butter into a pan and let it heat then pour your egg mixture.
  3. Make sure your heat is low not to burn the eggs.
  4. Use a spatula to turn the eggs.
