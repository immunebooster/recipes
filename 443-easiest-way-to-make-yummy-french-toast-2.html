French toast 2 instructions

  1. Cut out the center of your bread using a cookie cutter.
  2. Grease pan with oil and place bread to toast.
  3. At the center add in your whisked egg and allow to cook.
  4. Flip over after one side is done.
  5. Set aside. Take another slice and toast on pan.
  6. To the toast bread, spread letture add sardines, cabbage and lettuce slaw, tomatoes and onions.
  7. Close with the other slice.
  8. Cut diagonally and serve.
