Palm oil scrambled egg step by step

  1. Break eggs. Slice in the tomato, half onion, spices & seasoning..
  2. Whisk well to make sure everything is evenly distributed..
  3. Place a pan on the fire, add in palm oil. Slice in remaining half of the onion and saute..
  4. Pour in the egg mixture and stir continuously to break up the eggs until no more liquid is left..
  5. Turn off the heat and serve hot.
