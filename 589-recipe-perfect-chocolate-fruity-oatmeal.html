Ingredients of Chocolate fruity oatmeal

  1. Prepare 1 cup of oats.
  2. Prepare 2 cups of water.
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon of cocoa powder.
  4. Prepare 1 of generous handfull of frozen or fresh fruit.
  5. Prepare 1 of bannana (sliced).

Chocolate fruity oatmeal step by step

  1. Add the oats along with the water into a pot and bring to a boil..
  2. As the water and oats are heating up add the cocoa powder and the bannana..
  3. When the oats have expended and it is looking mushy add the frozen or fresh fruit and continue cooking for a few minutes..
