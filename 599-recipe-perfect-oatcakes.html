Ingredients of Oatcakes

  1. Prepare 85 g of coconut margarine.
  2. It's 110 g of plain flour.
  3. It's 1 tsp of baking powder.
  4. Prepare of cold water.
  5. Prepare 80 g of chopped walnuts.
  6. Prepare 20 g of maple syrup.
  7. It's 125 g of oats.
  8. It's 1 of vegan egg replacement (1 tbsp flax seeds + 3 tbsp water).
