Ingredients of French toast roll

  2. It's 5 tsp of peanut butter or chocolate spread.
  3. It's 5 tsp of finely chopped apple or one or two banana (sliced).
  4. You need 1 tsp of sugar.
  5. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  6. It's of as required Salt.
  7. You need of as required Oil.
  8. You need of as required Honey or chocolate sauce (for garnishing).

French toast roll step by step

  1. Firstly, remove the hard portion (sides) of the breads. Using roti roller, try to expand each bread slices slitely. Now take each bread slice, spread the peanut butter or chocolate spread at the edge (one side), fill 1 tsp chopped apple or 2-3 sliced bananas, fold the bread slice into a roll. Repeat for all the remaining bread slices..
  2. In a bowl, beat the egg, add salt and sugar, mix well..
  3. Heat a frying pan, add 1-2 tsp oil. Dip the bread rolls in the beaten egg mix and fry them..
  4. Serve after garnishing with honey or chocolate sauce on top..
