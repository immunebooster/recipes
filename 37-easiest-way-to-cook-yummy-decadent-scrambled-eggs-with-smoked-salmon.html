Decadent scrambled eggs with smoked salmon step by step

  1. Get a large, heavy bottomed frying pan on a medium heat to warm up.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add the cream and whisk until smooth.
  3. Toast the bread to your liking.
  4. Melt the butter in the pan, and cover the whole of the pan.
  5. Add the eggs to the melted butter and with a wooden fork, gently fold the eggs as they cook through.
  6. While the eggs are still runny around the edges, fold in the smoked salmon and turn off the heat..
  7. Remove pan from the heat and let the residual heat warm the salmon.
  8. Plate up with the egg and salmon mix on the toast, garnish with salt and pepper and sprinkle over the chopped chives.
