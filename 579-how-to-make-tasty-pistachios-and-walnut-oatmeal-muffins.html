Ingredients of Pistachios and walnut oatmeal muffins

  1. You need 1 cup of brown sugar.
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 cup of oats.
  3. You need 1 1/2 cup of wholemeal flour.
  4. It's 1 1/4 cup of milk.
  5. Prepare 2 tablespoon of ground flaxseed.
  6. Prepare 2 of apples shredded.
  7. Prepare 2 teaspoons of baking soda.
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of baking powder.
  9. Prepare 2 teaspoons of cinnamon.
  10. It's 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  11. It's 1/4 cup of oil.
  12. You need 1 teaspoon of cardamom powder or vanilla essence.
  13. It's 1 teaspoon of apple cider vinegar.
  14. You need 1/2 of x 2 cups chopped walnuts and pistachios.
  15. Prepare of Few strands of saffron.

Pistachios and walnut oatmeal muffins instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180C. In a bowl add milk and vinegar and leave for 10 minutes..
  2. In a bowl combine the flour, oats, brown sugar, ground flaxseed, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt. Add the milk, oil, cardamom and mix well. Add the shredded apples and the nuts. With a icecream scoop fill the muffin case with the batter and sprinkle nuts on top of muffins..

  4. Bake for 10 minutes at 180 C and then reduce the temperature to 160 C and bake for another 10 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean..
