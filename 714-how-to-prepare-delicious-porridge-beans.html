Porridge Beans.

You can have Porridge Beans using 10 ingredients and 11 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Porridge Beans

  1. It's 3 cups of Brown beans.
  2. Prepare 1 of Onion.
  3. It's of Crayfish powder.
  4. It's 4 of red peppers.
  5. It's of Cow skin.
  6. Prepare of Smoked fish.
  7. It's leaves of Pumpkin.
  8. Prepare of Salt.
  9. Prepare of Seasoning.
  10. Prepare of Plantain.

Porridge Beans step by step

  1. Prep all the ingredients present for the dish..
  2. Boil beans for an hour and if using a pressure cooker 30 minutes..
  3. Beans is ready.
  4. It's time to add all our ingredients together but first I preheat the pan and add my palm oil.
  5. Then I add the smoked fish, cow skin and leave to fry for some minutes before i finally add my blended red peppers and onions in with a 1 teaspoon of salt..
  6. When the sauce is ready I add my cooked beans to it and stir properly so the beans can absorb all the sauce..
  7. When the beans has absorbed the sauce properly then I add the crayfish powder to the mix and continue stirring.
  8. Now the porridge is ready I add the final ingredient which is my pumpkin leaves..
  9. Now my beans porridge is ready.
  10. The next step is to fry the plantain, which I slice and rub with a pinch of salt to taste more flavourful..
  11. Now our dish is ready to be served..
