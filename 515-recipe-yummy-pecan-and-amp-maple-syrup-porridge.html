Pecan & Maple Syrup Porridge step by step

  1. Put the oats in a bowl, pour on some milk and microwave for 3 minutes. If it’s too stodgy, add hot water or more milk and give it a good stir to loosen it up..
  2. Stir in the banana, top with your nuts, a sprinkle of cinnamon and a good glug of maple syrup..
