Ingredients of Apricot and tahini porridge

  1. You need 1/2 cup of steel cut oats.
  2. It's 1 1/2 cups of milk of choice - maybe more depending on your preferred consistency.
  3. Prepare Pinch of seasalt.
  4. Prepare 3 of apricots, cut into small pieces (fresh or frozen defrosted).
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of tahini.
  6. Prepare 1/2-1 tbsp of honey.
  7. Prepare of Ground cinnamon to sprinkle on top.

Apricot and tahini porridge instructions

  1. Heat oats and milk and salt in a pan (that has a lid for later)..
  2. When the oats/ milk start to boil, add the apricots, tahini and honey. Turn the heat down, cover and simmer for about 15 mins, stirring every now and then..
