Pancakes. Breakfast mustn't be regular always step by step

  1. Sieve flour and pour into a large bowl, make a hole in the centre and pour in the milk and eggs. Give the liquid mixture a quick whisk before incorporating the flour. Continue to whisk until you have a smooth batter..
  2. Now add sugar and salt in the right proportions with 1 tbsp vegetable oil and whisk thoroughly..
  3. Take a large frying pan, dip some kitchen roll in the oil and carefully wipe the inside of the pan. Heat the pan over a medium heat for 1 minute..
  4. Add just a tablespoonful of batter to the pan and immediately start swirling it round the pan to get a nice even layer..
  5. Cook the pancake for 30–40 seconds. Use a palette knife to lift the pancake carefully to look at the underside to check it is golden-brown before turning over. Cook the other side for approx 30– 40 seconds and transfer to a serving plate..
