Ingredients of Fried yam and plantain with scrambled egg sauce

  3. It's of Tomato 6 plums (chopped).
  4. You need of Onion 1 small.
  5. It's 4 pieces of Pepper.

  7. Prepare of Veg. Oil 1 cooking spoon.
  8. Prepare of Stock cube I cube.

Fried yam and plantain with scrambled egg sauce instructions

  1. Heat up your oil in a pan or deep fryer and fry the yam and plantain till done.
  2. Put 1 soup spoon of oil on a hot pan and fry the onion, tomato and pepper on medium heat for 4 minutes then add the stock cube..
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl and add it to the sauce on the fire and stir immediately to mix and the a little water just enough to steam and cover for a minute. Check if the taste is OK else add salt and it's ready..
