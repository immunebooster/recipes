Fried Yam and Scrambled eggs.

You can have Fried Yam and Scrambled eggs using 9 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you achieve that.

Ingredients of Fried Yam and Scrambled eggs

  1. You need of Yam.
  2. Prepare of Eggs.
  3. You need of Tomatoes.
  4. You need of Onions.
  5. Prepare of Groundnut oil + little margarine.
  6. Prepare of Chilli pepper.
  7. You need cubes of Seasoning.
  8. It's of Crayfish (optional).
  9. Prepare of Salt.

Fried Yam and Scrambled eggs instructions

  1. Peel and cut your yam nicely, to your desired shape for frying. Then pour in a bowl adding few pinches salt as well. (Depends on your yam quantity).
  2. Heat up your groundnut oil and when its hot, you put in your yam and fry for at least 4-5mins. And transfer in a sieve.
  3. For the scrambled eggs !!!!.....Heat up your pan and add your margarine + groundnut oil. Then allow to melt and get clearer..
  4. At this point, you introduce your onions and sâute for 2-3mins. Then introduce your sliced tomatoes and allow to fry well..
  5. Afterwards, you introduce your pepper, seasoning cubes, crayfish and salt. Then, the egg follows lastly..
  6. Stir the mixture for 3mins and its ready.
