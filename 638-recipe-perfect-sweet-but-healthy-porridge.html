Sweet but healthy porridge instructions

  1. Pour 200ml or 7 oz of milk into a measuring jug.
  2. Next add 100ml or 3.5 oz of cold water to the jug with the milk.
  3. Then add the heaped 1/2 cup of oats to the jug.
  4. Put it in the microwave for 2 mins then take out and stir.
  5. Put it in for another minute and then stir.
  6. Put it in for a final 30 secs or until it starts to rise.
  7. Take it out and add 1-2 tablespoons of honey then mix well.
  8. Finally add any desired toppings and serve!.
