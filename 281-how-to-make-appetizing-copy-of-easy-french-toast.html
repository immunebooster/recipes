Copy of Easy french toast step by step

  1. Beat eggs in a wide plate or beat eggs in a bowl then transfer it to a plate. It is easier to dip the sandwich bread in the beaten egg. Add 2 tbsp milk to make the eggs thinner. Whisk in protein powder.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan. add a dash of oil / butter into the pan..
  3. Dip a slice of bread into the beaten egg both side. You may dip it longer or just to cover the sandwich bread..
  4. Transfer the sandwich bread into heated pan, bake it for 1 minute each side until golden brown..
  5. Repeat with second piece of sandwich bread.
  6. Serve it warm with mashed banana and a tsp of jam (or another delicious topping of your choice!).
