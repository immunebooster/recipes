Ingredients of Scrambled egg on Hummus toast

  1. Prepare 2 of eggs.
  2. Prepare 1 of small tomato chopped fine.
  3. You need of Few sprigs of Basil leaves.
  4. You need 2 slices of bread.
  5. Prepare 2 Tbsp of olive oil.
  6. Prepare 1-2 pods of garlic chopped fine.
  7. Prepare 4-5 Tbsp of Hummus.
  8. Prepare of Black pepper powder as per taste.
  9. It's of Salt as per taste.

Scrambled egg on Hummus toast step by step

  1. In a cutting board chop the tomatoes fine, chop the garlic fine. Chop the Basil leaves fine.
  2. Whisk the eggs really well so you get a froth. In a non stick add 1 Tbsp olive oil and add the eggs and keep stirring so that it becomes scrambled.
  3. In a small bowl to the chopped tomatoes add chopped garlic, chopped basil and salt and pepper and set aside.
  4. In a non stick wok add a little olive oil and toast the bread slices.
  5. Add the hummus in both the slices, add chopped tomatoes and scrambled eggs. Assemble and enjoy.
