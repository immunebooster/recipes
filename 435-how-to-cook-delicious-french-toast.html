French toast instructions

  1. Break your eggs in a different bowl grate your scotch bonnet and onions add your curry and seasonings and mix.
  2. Put your milk powder in a different bowl.
  3. Put your pan on low heat add little oil deep your slice bread into the milk powder then into the egg and put in the pan when is brown you turn to the other side you repeat the process untill you finish.
  4. Enjoy with tea quacker oats or anything of your choice.
