Tomato scrambled eggs #festiveseasondish-Nairobi instructions

  1. On a heated pot, add oil then onions. Stir until they are golden brown.
  2. Add tomatoes and capsicum. Cover your pot and let them cook until tender..
  3. Add the whisked eggs and salt then mix. Cover your pot and let it cook for 2-3 minutes..
  4. Add the coriander leaves then mix. Let it cook for a minute or two..
  5. Serve for breakfast or with ugali or avocado..
  6. Enjoy.
