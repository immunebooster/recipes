Ingredients of Chickpea flour porridge/besan ka halwa ðŸ¥£

  1. You need 2 1/2 tbsp of chickpea flour.
  2. You need 4 tsp of unsalted butter or ghee.
  3. It's 2 tbsp of sugar or jaggery.
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup of water.
  5. It's Pinch of cardamom powder.

Besan ka halwa is a gluten-free Indian dessert made of chickpea flour, ghee, jaggery, milk, milk solids and nuts. It is soft, aromatic and easy to make. Plus, it's very comforting as a drink called besan ka sheera if you have a cold or cough. Lots of reasons to love this sweet treat!

Chickpea flour porridge/besan ka halwa ðŸ¥£ step by step

  1. Saute chickpea flour alone in a pan on low flame for 7-10 minutes, until nice aroma comes..
  2. Prepare sugar mixture separately by adding sugar/jaggery and water together. Mix until sugar/jaggery dissolve in water completely. I have used jaggery as shown in the picture, but you could also use sugar of your choice..
  3. Put unsalted butter/ghee in a pan, add roasted chickpea flour to it and whisk it for 5-7 minutes on medium heat..
  4. Pour the sugar mixture and cardamom powder, once butter and flour mixed well without lumps and turning brown. Whisk until everything holds together..
  5. Serve warm..
