Pasta with scrambled eggs instructions

  1. Add water into the cooking pot and let it boil with salt in it, Break the pasta into half and add them into the boiling water. Boil for about 4-5minutes.
  2. Drain the remaining water after it has boiled and cool the pasta into cold water..
  3. In a pan add the cooking oil, then add the onions, tomatoes, chilli, salt, garlic and ginger powder into the pan let them get half cooked for about a minute as you mix.
  4. Beat your two eggs into a cup stir it and add it into your mixture.
  5. Then immediately add the pasta and start mixing everything together as they cook for about two minutes and it's ready to serve!.
