Ingredients of Banana Bread Porridge

  1. You need 40 g of porridge oats.
  2. You need 180 ml of milk/water.
  3. You need of Semi-skimmed or dairy free (Almond works well).
  4. It's 1 of large banana: ½ mashed and ½ sliced (if want caramelised bananas).
  5. Prepare 2 tsp of maple syrup/honey.
  6. It's 1/4 tsp of cinnamon.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract.
  8. It's Pinch of salt.
  9. Prepare of Small handful of chopped almonds.
  10. It's of To serve.
  11. Prepare of Small handful toasted flaked almonds.
  12. It's of Caramelized bananas.

Banana Bread Porridge instructions

  1. Heat the oats, milk/water, mashed banana, chopped almonds, cinnamon and vanilla in a saucepan over a medium-low heat..
  2. Cook for 5 minutes until the oats are cooked and the texture is as desired (add more milk if you want it thinner)..
  3. Whilst the porridge is cooking, cook the caramelised bananas. In a non-stick frying pan, place the sliced bananas and cover with the maple syrup. Cook over a medium heat, flipping gently occasionally, until golden brown and soft..
  4. Pour into a bowl and serve with the toasted flaked almonds and a dash of honey/maple syrup if desired..
