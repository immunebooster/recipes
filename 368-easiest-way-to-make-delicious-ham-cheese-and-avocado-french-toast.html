Ingredients of Ham Cheese and Avocado French Toast

  1. Prepare 4 slices of bread.
  2. Prepare 2 slices of cheese, or to taste (I used colby jack cheese).
  3. Prepare 4 slices of ham, or to taste.
  4. It's 1 of avocado, pitted and cut into thin slices.
  5. You need 2 tbs of pickled jalapeno, chopped.
  6. You need 2 tbs of mayo.
  7. Prepare 1 tsp of onion powder.
  8. You need to taste of salt and pepper.

Ham Cheese and Avocado French Toast instructions

  1. Crack two eggs into a shallow plate and whisk for about 10 seconds. Set aside..
  2. Line the fryer basket with grill mat or a sheet of lightly greased aluminum foil..
  3. Spread some mayonnaise to one side of each slice of bread. Then, assemble the sandwich by putting ham, cheese, jalapeno and avocado in between two slices of bread..
  4. Dip the both sides of the sandwich in egg mixture, each side for about 3 seconds. Put the sandwiches inside the fryer basket..
