French toast with bacon and caramelized bananas instructions

  1. French toast - in a bowl whisk egg, milk, salt together. Heat pan with oil. Dip bread in egg mixture and fry few minutes on each side until golden..
  2. Fry bacon in bit of oil until crispy.
  3. Caramelized banana - slice bananas. Melt butter in pan. Fry banana and add sugar. Fry few minutes until slightly sticky..
